Our History

Our Humble Beginning

Our church started as a small group shortly after a crusade held by Dr. Lewis, who was the president of the SDA Conference at the time on Samaritan Hill.

April 2011

Organized as a Company

We were officially organized into a company with a membership of approximately 39 dedicated members.

December 3, 2016

Established as a Church

Our community reached a significant milestone when we were officially established as a church.

Today

Growing in Faith and Community

We continue to grow and serve, currently with 84 members in our church family.
