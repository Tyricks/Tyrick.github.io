Church Departments

The Seventh-Day Adventist Church is a religious organization with many different departments, each serving a unique purpose in our church community and outreach efforts. Explore our departments below to learn more about their missions and activities.

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry focuses on nurturing the spiritual growth of our youngest members through age-appropriate Bible lessons, activities, and events designed to build a strong foundation of faith.

Music Ministry

The Music Ministry enhances our worship services through inspirational music, choir performances, and musical training. We believe music is a powerful way to connect with God and uplift the congregation.

Community Service

Our Community Service department organizes outreach programs, food drives, and support services for those in need within our local community, following Christ's example of service and compassion.

Sabbath School

The Sabbath School department provides Bible study groups for all ages, fostering spiritual growth, fellowship, and deeper understanding of Scripture through interactive lessons and discussion.

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry engages teenagers and young adults through relevant programs, retreats, and activities that strengthen their faith and provide a supportive Christian community.

Mission & Evangelism

This department coordinates local and international mission efforts, evangelistic campaigns, and supports the global mission of the Seventh-Day Adventist Church to share the gospel worldwide.
